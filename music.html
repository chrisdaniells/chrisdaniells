Alt-Rock Demo Album

It's part of my any 2018 goals to finish an 11/12 track album of relatively-polished-yet-still-scruffy demo recordings.

The album has been in the works since early 2017, and with only two or three tracks more to record and time being the only limiting factor, I anticipate the recordings to be finished by the middle of year. After that it'll need proepr mixing, mastering and perhaps a bit of fine tuning.

Here is the playlist of demos of the demos that have been recorded so far. As you will hear, they vary in quality depending on how recently I recorded them (they are not in chronological order, and I've learned a lot about audio engineering along the way).

Retro Sci-Fi Movie Score

I've recently starting creating a retro shynthwave-ish Sci-Fi concept movie score. It's a mixture of old and new: the first track (which is a collection of three shorts) is new, and made for the purpose of the score, whilst the other tracks are previous songs I have made which fit in nicely with the concept.

This is a new project, so there will be more to come!
